Summary

The above plot helps to quickly visualize the impact of implementing predictive analytics to perform better customer segmentation and target marketing, using your current text marketing.

Each circle represents the revenues in a given month for each year. The larger and darker the circle, the higher the sales.

For comparison, take a look at the size and color of the circles after the COVID pandemic shutdown in March 2020.

Now look at the size and color of the circles after September 2021, when we started using computer modeling for predicting sales, and beating those predictions using more accurate customer segmentation and target marketing
.
As a result, you have had 4 record months with highest sales compared to the same time period prior to the pandemic. Those are Nov 2021, Mar 2022, Apr 2022, May 2022 and are illustrated by clicking on either the ‘Plots’ or ‘Records’ links above, for more details underneath each plot.

Also, Oct 2021, and Dec 2021 were the second best months in terms of sales when compared to the same time periods before the pandemic. The illustrations and details for those will be added to this website, soon.

In summary, as a result of integrating your loyalty rewards system and your POS system, and implementing computer based prediction analysis, your sales have improved by 32%, profit margins by 6%, and the contribution of loyalty rewards customers (VIP) to your top line sales has increased from 20% to 28%.
